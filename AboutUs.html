Happy Pup Groomers
Established in 2015
Looking for a luxurious and wellness-focused dog grooming experience in San Diego? Look no further than Happy Pup Groomers! Our boutique dog resort and spa is designed with your pup's ultimate comfort, relaxation, and well-being in mind.

Our lavish cage-free suites and spacious play area are perfect for giving your furry friend the pampering they deserve. We also offer a range of a la carte enrichment activities and spa services to keep your pup happy and healthy. We take health and safety seriously, which is why we have advanced water and air filtration systems, soundproof walls, and puppy peek cams to ensure your pup is in a caring and stress-free environment.


Happy Pup Groomers was established in November 2015 as a family-owned and operated business. Determined to provide a better service, Our love for dogs and the demand for quality dog care in our community led us to start small business and grow into a fully recognized pet groomer. Our goal is to become the gold standard for local pet care in the community.
